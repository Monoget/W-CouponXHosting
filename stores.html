About Macy’s

Founded in 1858 in New York City, New York, Macy’s has a long and prestigious history as one of the nation’s premier department stores. Today, with over 760 stores and a thriving online store, the chain offers a great array of products, including designer goods, fine jewelry, fashion, shoes, home appliances and more. During the holiday season, they often stock some of the most popular toys and gifts, too. The brand aims to cultivate regular shoppers with frequent deals, discounts and sales, which is wonderful news for bargain hunters with an eye for the finer things in life.
